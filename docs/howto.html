How to Use SipSmartAI

SipSmartAI helps you monitor alcohol consumption and stay within your safe limit.

  1. Create an account and fill in your profile details.
  2. Start a drinking session from the home screen.
  3. Tap a drink icon to log each drink consumed.
  4. Track your streak and weekly Smart Points on the progress page.
